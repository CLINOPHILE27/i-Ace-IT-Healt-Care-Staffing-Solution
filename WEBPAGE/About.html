About Us


i-ACE It Health Care Staffing Solutions is a Nursing and Healthcare Agency that aims to provide the best healthcare services across Manitoba, Canada.
The Agency was founded in 2022 by a group of well- dynamic nursing professionals. As pillars, we believe that the epitome of nursing care is best delivered by
healthcare providers, who we called “ ACERS”. Our company covenants to achieve excellence in Arts of Care and delivery of exemplary services to our partners in health, staff, clients and their families.



Mission


Our company is committed to bridging the gap of staffing shortages across Manitoba through delivery of healthcare services in various healthcare settings,
in partnership with highly skilled healthcare providers.



Vission


Our vision is to be widely recognized as the staffing benchmark in providing excellent care to our clients through our qualified,
empowered and highly motivated healthcare providers.
